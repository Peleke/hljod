## Phase 1: The Beat (Lessons 0-7)

All theory concepts identical. Repertoire recast:

| L | Title | Theory (unchanged) | Old Vehicle | New Repertoire | Ðirðug Parallel |
|---|-------|-------------------|-------------|---------------|----------------|
| 0 | First Noise | patterns, mini-notation, s(), note(), stack() | generic | **Zelda Chest Jingle** (Kondo) + **Mario Coin** (Kondo) | Sparse koto: 3-4 notes in silence |
| 1 | The Break | DnB groove, 170bpm, half-time snare, kick | generic DnB | **Metroid Brinstar** (Hirokazu Tanaka) + **Mega Man 2 Wily Stage 1** (Takashi Tateishi) | Slow deep pulse under the koto |
| 2 | The Bass | waveforms, ADSR, lpf, hpf, reese | generic bass | **Skyrim Dragonborn** (Jeremy Soule) + **Minecraft Subwoofer Lullaby** (C418) | Filtered bass drone, the ocean |
| 3 | Layers | euclidean, ghost notes, pan, stereo | generic layers | **Chrono Trigger Wind Scene** (Mitsuda) + **Stardew Valley Spring** (ConcernedApe) | Second voice, shakuhachi ghost |
| 4 | Atmosphere | reverb, delay, room, pads, chords | phonk pivot | **Dark Souls Firelink Shrine** + **Zelda BotW Temple of Time** (Kondo/Wakai) | Cathedral of space. THE JAPANESE AMBIENT LESSON. |
| 5 | Movement | every, sometimes, jux, off, transforms | generic | **Zelda Lost Woods** (Kondo, Mixolydian) + **Undertale Megalovania** (Toby Fox) | Parameters shift, the piece evolves |
| 6 | The Drop | cat(), arrangement, silence, builds | generic | **FF7 One Winged Angel** (Uematsu) + **Hollow Knight Sealed Vessel** (Larkin) | Beginning, middle, end |
| 7 | Chopping | chop, slice, speed, loopAt, amen | amen break | **Amen break** (stays) + chopped game samples | Texture and grit. Piece complete. |

**L4 is the pivot.** This is where the dark Japanese ambient aesthetic from the reference track lands. Firelink Shrine IS that vibe. The lesson teaches reverb, delay, room, and pad synthesis through a piece that sounds like the koto/shakuhachi track you downloaded. The Ðirðug parallel composition should feel like the reference by the end of this lesson.

**L7 capstone:** "The North Atlantic" complete. 60-90 seconds. Dark ambient koto. Guðríður's crossing.
