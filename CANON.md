### Priority for Phase 2 full recreations

These need note-accurate Strudel transcriptions:

1. **Mario Overworld** (L8a) — C major. The melody everyone knows.
2. **Lost Woods / Saria's Song** (L8b) — full recreation, not approximation.
3. **Gerudo Valley** (L8a) — minor. Full flamenco melody.
4. **FF Prelude** (L8b) — the pentatonic arpeggio.
5. **Lavender Town** (L8b) — the creepy one.
6. **Terra's Theme** (L8e) — suspensions and non-chord tones.
7. **Persona 5: Last Surprise** (L9b) — ii-V-I.
8. **Ace Attorney: Cross-Examination** (L9e) — walking bass.
9. **Zelda Main Theme** across games (L9f) — motivic development.
